RXJS Operators

Operators are RXJS tools that can transform the data being passed through the data stream

Operator take until example

const observable1 = of(1,2,3);
this.observable1.pipe(takeUntil(this.stop$).subscribe((value) => console.log(value));

The take until operator takes the values from the observable until the observable passed as parameter emits a value
